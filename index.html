Tabletop RPG Rules Overview

  1. Dice Rolling: Use dice rolls to determine the outcomes of your actions.

  2. Combat: Engage in turn-based battles using strategic positioning, spells, and abilities.

  3. Storytelling: Immerse yourself in the narrative led by the Game Master (GM).

  4. Magic & Abilities: Utilize magic spells and special abilites according to game rules.

  5. Inventory: Manage your character's equipment, items, and treasures.

  6. Experience & Leveling: Gain experience points (XP) to improve your character's skills and abilities

  7. Role-Playing: Act in-character to make decisions, interact with NPCs, and explore the game world.

  8. Rule Modifications: Adjust rules to fit the group's playstyle for maximum fun.
